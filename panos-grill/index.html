What We Do

Our Services

Everything you need, handled by experienced local professionals.

🔥

Wood-Fire Grilled Proteins

Prime cuts and whole fish are seared over an open flame using traditional Greek grilling techniques for deep char and smoky flavor.

🥗

Mezze & Shared Plates

House-made hummus, tzatziki, spanakopita, and seasonal small plates are prepared daily using imported Mediterranean ingredients.

🥩

Gyro & Souvlaki Counter

Hand-stacked vertical rotisserie gyro and marinated souvlaki skewers are carved and assembled to order on fresh-baked pita.

🍷

Curated Wine & Cocktail Program

Our beverage list features Greek and domestic wines, ouzo-based cocktails, and craft selections that complement our bold flavor profiles.

🎉

Private Dining & Event Catering

We offer prix fixe menus, family-style banquet service, and off-site catering with full chafing dish setup for groups of 20 to 200.

📦

Online Ordering & Delivery

Our streamlined online ordering system offers contactless pickup and third-party delivery with packaging designed to maintain food temperature and presentation.
